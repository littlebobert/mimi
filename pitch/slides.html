The stakes

You have to improve your Japanese

  • Or you lose job prospects
  • You miss real connections with people around you
  • You might eventually have to go home

The pressure is real. The learning has to fit real life.

Problem

Translation tools help you understand, not remember

  • The best vocab is personal: from your calls, dinners, live moments
  • Import tools (LingQ, Migaku) need content you prepared ahead of time
  • Tutor call, friends speaking JP, real world: no transcript, nothing sticks

Solution

Machine intelligence for live audio

  • Phone mic → Gemini Live → bilingual subtitles in real time
  • A study coach reviews the original audio after the conversation
  • It cleans up the deck using mistakes, hesitation, and words you already know

Roadmap

From live help to an agentic study pipeline

  • Today: listen, translate, tap words, capture flashcards
  • Next: post-session coach analyzes audio and fixes the deck
  • Later: dictionary, web, and Anki tools loop until the study plan is right
